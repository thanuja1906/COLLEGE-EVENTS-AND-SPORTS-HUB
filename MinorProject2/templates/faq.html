Common Questions

Browse through our frequently asked questions below

What are your booking hours?

Bookings are available during these hours only:

  • Morning: 8:00 AM to 10:00 AM
  • Evening: 3:00 PM to 9:00 PM

No bookings are required between 10:00 AM to 3:00 PM as courts are available for walk-in use during this time.

How can I book a court?

Court bookings can be made through our online system during booking hours. Simply select your preferred date and time slot, then complete the booking form.

What is the cancellation policy?

Cancellations must be made at least 2 hours before your booking time. Repeated no-shows may result in booking restrictions.

Can I use the courts without booking?

Yes, courts are available for walk-in use between 10:00 AM to 3:00 PM on a first-come, first-served basis. No prior booking is needed during these hours.
